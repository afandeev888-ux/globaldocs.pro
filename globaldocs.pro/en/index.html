Our Services

Everything you need to get your documents from Russia, processed and delivered to your doorstep.

Document Retrieval

We provide ZAGS document retrieval services:

  • birth certificate; birth certificate (form 4)
  • marriage certificate, marriage record (form 5, form 6 — in case of divorce)
  • divorce certificate, divorce record
  • certificate (form 15)
  • name change certificate
  • death certificate

If needed, we can also obtain court decisions, police clearance certificates, and arrange an apostille.

Apostille

We arrange apostille certification for your Russian documents so they are legally recognized in countries that are members of the 1961 Hague Apostille Convention.

Translation

Professional translation of your documents from Russian into English, German, Hebrew, Spanish, French and other languages by certified translators.

Consular Legalization

For documents destined for countries that are not part of the Hague Convention, we handle the full consular legalization process through the relevant authorities.

International Delivery

Secure and trackable delivery of your original documents to any country using trusted courier services with full insurance and tracking numbers.

Notary Copies

We prepare notarized copies and certified translations of your Russian documents, ensuring they meet the legal requirements of the receiving country.
