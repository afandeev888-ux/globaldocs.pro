Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal technical information necessary to operate and secure our website.

This may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of access

This data is collected automatically when you visit the website.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To ensure website security
  • To monitor performance and stability
  • To improve website functionality and user experience

We do not use your data to identify you personally.

3. Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics provided by Cloudflare to understand how visitors interact with our website.

Cloudflare Web Analytics:

  • does not use cookies
  • does not track users across websites
  • collects only aggregated and anonymized data (Cloudflare)

This includes basic metrics such as:

  • Page views
  • General location (country level)
  • Device type
  • Performance metrics

This helps us improve our website without identifying individual users.

4. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for its operation and security.

We do not use cookies for:

  • Tracking
  • Advertising
  • Marketing

5. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare as a Content Delivery Network (CDN) and security provider.

Cloudflare may process limited technical data such as IP addresses to:

  • Protect the website from attacks
  • Improve loading speed
  • Ensure reliability

6. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us.
