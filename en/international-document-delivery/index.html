Why Document Delivery Matters

When you arrange documents from Russia — apostille, legalization, translation, or obtaining new documents from civil registry offices — eventually the question arises: how do you receive these documents? Especially if you are abroad. Regular mail doesn't guarantee the safety of important documents, and delivery times can stretch to weeks. We solve this problem by offering professional international delivery through trusted courier services.

Our clients are people around the world who trust us to obtain and process documents from Russia. Once all work is completed, we organize delivery of original documents to your specified address. This is the final and very important stage of the entire process, and we approach it with maximum responsibility. Every envelope is insured, every shipment is tracked, and you receive notifications about delivery status.

How We Organize Delivery

The document delivery process is organized so that you don't have to worry about the safety and location of your envelope. From the moment documents are ready until they're in your hands — we control every stage.

1. Documents Ready

After completing all work (apostille, legalization, translation), we send you photos of the ready documents for verification. You confirm everything is correct, and we begin packaging.

2. Packaging and Documentation

Documents are packed in a protective envelope with bubble wrap. We prepare the declaration, insurance, and all necessary documents for international shipping. We select the optimal courier service.

3. Handover to Courier

The courier picks up the shipment from our Moscow office. We receive a tracking number and immediately send it to you. From this moment, you can track the envelope's movement on the courier's website.

4. Delivery and Handover

The courier delivers documents to the specified address. A signature is required upon receipt. If you're not home, the courier will leave a notice with instructions for redelivery or pickup at a collection point.

Insurance and Guarantees

We understand that the documents we send are often irreplaceable and have enormous value for our clients. Birth certificates, diplomas, court decisions — losing such documents can create serious problems. That's why every shipment is insured.
