About Global Docs Pro

Since 2015, we have been helping people around the world obtain, legalize and deliver documents from Russia — quickly, reliably and without hassle.

Our Story

Global Docs Pro was founded in 2015 in Moscow with a simple but important mission — to help people who have left Russia obtain the documents they need without having to travel back. At first, these were individual requests from friends and acquaintances: obtain a birth certificate, arrange an apostille, translate a certificate.

Over the years, demand only grew. People from Germany, the USA, Israel, Spain, Canada and dozens of other countries turned to us for help. We expanded our team, established relationships with government agencies and courier services, and refined our processes. Today, Global Docs Pro is a full-fledged company with over 9 years of experience in international document services.

We are proud that hundreds of clients trust us with their documents. Every request is not just a bureaucratic procedure for us, but real help to a person in solving vital matters — from obtaining a visa to family reunification.
